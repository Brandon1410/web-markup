Is all the activites location walkable/cyclable?

As you can see by the google image of Nieuwkoop , So all the activity sites will be at least 500 - 1000 meter away from the central of Nieuwkoop. With this information you can think either you want to walk, drive or even cycle to the activity site you want to go.

To the Marina Plaszitch

With looking by the image on the side, you can see that from the central of Nieuwkoop to the Marina Plaszitch ther distance between them is only 600m and it can take you 2 minute by bike and 8 minutes by walking. Doesnt look hard right?

To the Sport Centrum

And same goes to this image where the distance between them is a little farther then the previous image, the distance from the Nieuwkoop to Nieuwkoop sport centrum is 1,3km. It take only 6 minutes by bike from the central of the town to the sport centrum while it take 18 minutes by walking. Those who really like enjoy walking will be pleasant with the route as you will cross a football pitch where many kids will be training football.
