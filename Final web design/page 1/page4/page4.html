What is the best weather to be in Nieuwkoop?

I would say summer is the best weather to be here in Nieewkoop, As all the activities will be outdoor you wouldnt want to do outdoor activities in the rain do you? unless you are a person who likes cold and rainy outdoor activities then it wouldnt be a problem for you!.

As to get back to the main topic, summer is best weather to be in Nieuwkoop because activities to do here are all outdoor and it is nice to be in a boat in the hot sun and your body sweating thus you will jump in the lake of Nieuwkoop to cool down.
